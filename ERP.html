Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a custom ERP system cost?

The cost of a custom ERP system can vary greatly depending on the specific requirements and complexity of the system. On average, the cost can range from $50,000 to $100,000 or more. Factors that can affect the cost include the number of modules needed, the level of customization required, the size and expertise of the development team, and the hosting and maintenance costs. It’s important to note that while a custom ERP system can be a significant investment, it can also provide substantial benefits in terms of efficiency, accuracy, and flexibility.

How much time does it require to build an ERP solution?

The time required to build an ERP solution can also vary greatly, depending on the same factors that affect cost. A basic ERP system with a few modules might take 3-6 months to develop, while a more complex system with many modules could take a year or more. It’s important to work with a development team that can provide a realistic timeline and stick to it, to minimize disruption to your business operations.

What pricing models are available for ERP system development?

There are several pricing models commonly used for ERP system development. Here are some of the most popular ones:

  • Fixed Price: With a fixed price model, you agree to pay a set amount for the development of your ERP system. This model is best suited for projects with clearly defined requirements and timelines.

  • Time and Materials: With a time and materials model, you pay for the actual time and resources used in developing your ERP system. This model is more flexible than a fixed price model and can be adjusted as requirements change.

  • Retainer: With a retainer model, you pay a set monthly fee for ongoing development and maintenance services. This model is best suited for projects that require ongoing support and updates.
